Edukeen is a Sri Lankan and UK based Education Consultancy Agency that will help you and advise you to find the right university based on your individual requirement. We will guide you through every step of the way.

We help students find Universities

Based on students interest area of study we find you the best universities to accomplish your goals. We offer courses ranging from undergraduate to postgraduate. Payments are directly paid to university giving you highest transparency without charging any fee for services. We can offer you attractive scholarships based on your achievements in studies.

We find accommodation and work for students

Based on the students university we can help you find accommodation close by, job opportunities, airport pick up and help you settle down in the UK.
